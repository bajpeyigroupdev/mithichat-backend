Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 15, 2025

Meethi Chat Voice Chat Room ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, username, profile picture, email address (for login).
  • Usage Data: Interactions throughout the app, call logs metadata.
  • Device Information: Device ID, IP address, OS version for security and analytics.
  • Images/Audio: Profile photos and voice chats are processed to provide the service.

2. User Generated Content (UGC) Policy (Strict)

We are committed to maintaining a safe community. By using our App, you agree to the following:

  • Zero Tolerance: We have zero tolerance for objectionable content (nudity, hate speech, harassment) or abusive users.
  • Reporting & Blocking: Users can flag objectionable content or block abusive users immediately via the 'Report' or 'Block' buttons on profiles/chats.
  • Review Process: Our moderation team reviews reported content within 24 hours. Violators will be suspended or banned permanently.

3. Children's Privacy

Strictly 18+: Our App is NOT intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from children without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

4. Data Usage & Security

We use your data to facilitate voice connection, manage your account, and ensure safety. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information.
